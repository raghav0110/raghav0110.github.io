Datacenter Management through Smartphones

I am currently the lead on a project that aims to build a scalable system for Datacenter management through mobile phones. This project is a collaboration with IBM Research and was motivated by their need for a mobile system for datacenter management. My contribution towards the project includes designing the system architecture, adding novel components to the system, researching related work by reading various research papers, and writing a research paper that was submitted to the International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS). The paper is currently under review and be found here.
